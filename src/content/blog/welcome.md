# We Have Evolved.

In 2021, we launched **SoPlugged** with a simple mission: to help Black-owned businesses get found. We built a directory, hosted pop-ups, and created a community that spanned from Halifax to Vancouver.

But as we watched our members grow, we realized that "being found" wasn't enough.

## Enter Compound

**Compound Accelerator** is our answer to that need. We are no longer just a list of names. We are an engine for growth.

We have moved away from broad "support" to focused **acceleration**.
